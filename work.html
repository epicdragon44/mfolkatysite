Not One More


We cannot allow one more person to be killed by senseless gun violence.
We cannot allow one more person to experience the pain of losing a loved one.
We cannot allow one more family to wait for a call or a text that never comes.
We cannot allow the normalization of gun violence to continue.
We must create a safe and compassionate nation for all of us.
​

What's the national mission?

To harness the power of young people across the country to fight for sensible gun violence prevention policies that saves lives.

​

What's the MFOL Katy Mission?

​

Our goals align with the national organization, in that we hope to end gun violence in America by empowering the youth, the voters, and encouraging democratic power that disrupts the false narrative of the NRA. To do this, we hope to create grassroots support across the sprawling suburbs of Houston, America's 4th largest city, an area that suffers no less than other cities from gun violence and the specter of domestic terrorism and suicide, but which is neglected on paper due to its location. Founded by minority high-school students across the socioeconomic spectrum of the area in KatyISD with broad support, we hope to induce major societal change from the ground up. ​ ​
